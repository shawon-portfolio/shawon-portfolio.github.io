Rupantor App Details

Rupantor App (Olympiad Management App)

Rupantor is a comprehensive Olympiad registration and management system, built to streamline the entire process of managing academic competitions. From participant registration and fee management to event scheduling and result publication, Rupantor simplifies complex workflows with its user-friendly interface.

Developed with Angular, .NET, and Azure, the system ensures scalability, robust performance, and secure data handling. Its cloud integration enables real-time updates, automated notifications, and efficient record management, making it an ideal solution for organizing Olympiad events with ease and professionalism.
